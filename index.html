About Me

Hi, I'm Yash Rajbhandari

I am a UI/UX designer skilled in Figma, specializing in wireframing, prototyping, and mockups. My background in sales and IT support helps me approach design with a user-focused, problem-solving mindset.

I am proficient in color theory and typography, and have hands-on experience designing interactive prototypes for web and mobile platforms. I enjoy collaborating with developers and product managers to ensure design feasibility and user satisfaction.

My education in social work and sociology gives me a unique perspective on user needs and behavior, which I bring into every project.
